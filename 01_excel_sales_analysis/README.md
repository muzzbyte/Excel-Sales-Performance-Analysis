## Business Problem
The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance data to identify
revenue trends, top-performing products, and underperforming regions.

## Dataset
Sales transaction dataset containing order date, product category,
region, revenue, and profit metrics.

## Tools Used
- Excel / Google Sheets
- Pivot Tables
- Lookup Functions
- Basic Statistical Analysis

## Key Analysis
- Monthly and quarterly sales trends
- Regional revenue comparison
- Product-wise profitability analysis

## Insights
- Certain regions consistently underperformed despite high sales volume
- A small number of products contributed to a large share of total profit

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-margin products
- Re-evaluate pricing strategy in low-profit regions
